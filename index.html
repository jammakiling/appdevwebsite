MARIO KART ARCADE GP 2

The first Mario Kart Arcade GP was so successful that Namco (now known as Namco Bandai) decided to release a follow-up.

Arcade GP 2 wasn’t a proper ‘sequel’ in the true sense of the word, it’s better described as an enhanced version of the original which added more of everything. Four more tracks were added: two Yoshi-themed ones and two stadium-themed ones.
