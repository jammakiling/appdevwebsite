About Daisy!

Find out more about the character!

Princess Daisy (or simply just Daisy) is a character who first appeared in Super Mario Land. She is good friends with Mario, Luigi, Yoshi, Peach, Rosalina, Toadette, and the rest of the "good guys". Daisy has an apparent love interest in Luigi as shown in Daisy Circuit's fountains depicting the two. She serves as the second female in the Mario series. Daisy make her debut in Mario Kart in the series' fourth title, Mario Kart: Double Dash!!, as Peach's default partner.
